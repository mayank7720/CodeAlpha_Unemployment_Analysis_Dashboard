# Slope Skill Guidelines

When this skill is active, apply the following practices to data dashboards:
1. **Trend & Slope Visualizations**:
   - Highlight the rate of change (slope) in time-series data. Display metrics showing whether a trend is accelerating or decelerating.
   - Use color-coded indicators for trend directions (green for favorable changes, red for unfavorable changes).
2. **Regression & Trendlines**:
   - Include visual regression lines (trendlines) on scatter or line charts to clearly convey direction and rate of change.
   - Show mathematical values of slopes (e.g. rate per month) in details/tooltips.
3. **Comparative Dynamics**:
   - Compare slopes across different cohorts (e.g. comparing the rate of change of Rural vs. Urban unemployment).
