## Key Findings

### 1. COVID-19 Pandemic Shock
A dramatic surge in unemployment was observed during the national lockdown months (March - May 2020).
- **Pre-COVID Average Unemployment Rate:** 9.23%
- **Lockdown Period Average Unemployment Rate:** 18.71% (An increase of **102.7%**)
- **Peak Single Record:** **75.85%** in **Puducherry** on **30-04-2020**.
